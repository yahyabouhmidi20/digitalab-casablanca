## What AI Automation Actually Means

Forget the hype. Here's what AI automation does in plain terms:

### AI Chatbots
Software that answers customer questions on your website, WhatsApp, or Instagram—24/7, in seconds.

### Automated Messaging
Sequences that follow up with leads, confirm appointments, and re-engage inactive customers—without you typing a word.

### Workflow Automation
Systems that move data between your tools, assign tasks, send notifications, and eliminate manual busywork.
